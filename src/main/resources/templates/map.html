Welcome to the Map Application

Steps to Access the Map Application Interface:

  • Step 1: Log in to your account using valid credentials.
  • Step 2: Navigate to the "Map Access" section in the dashboard.
  • Step 3: Enter the required location details in the search bar.
  • Step 4: Use available filters to customize the view.
  • Step 5: Click on specific markers for additional information.
  • Step 6: Explore additional features, such as layers and analytics tools.
