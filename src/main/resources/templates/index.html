About Us

The Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC) is the premier R&D organization under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY), focusing on research in IT and Electronics. Established in 1988, C-DAC has been at the forefront of supercomputing development in India, starting with the PARAM series. We specialize in diverse areas, including Indian language computing, health informatics, and software technologies.
